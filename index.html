About Me

WHO I AM

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Oklahoma State University, working in the Complex System Lab.

My research is centered on advancing methodologies in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Pattern Recognition.

Currently, I am working on addressing the challenges of modality imbalance in multimodal training and on refining transfer learning techniques—such as knowledge distillation across different applications.

Before my Ph.D., I gained industry experience as a Data Scientist & ML Engineer at Neovotech Ltd.

I also contributed to research as a Research Assistant at Apurba-NSU R&D Lab, working closely with Dr. Nabeel Mohammed.

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from North South University (NSU), Bangladesh.

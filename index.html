About Me

WHO I AM

I'm doing PhD in Computer Science at Oklahoma State University.

Working at Complex System Lab under the supervision of Dr. Arunkumar Bagavathi on the broad area of Big Data Analysis.

Previously I worked as a Lecturer in Computer Science and Engineering department at University of Asia Pacific.

I mainly conducted the theory course of Machine Learning and the lab course of Computer Graphics.

I received Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology.

My research interest includes Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Pattern Recognition.
